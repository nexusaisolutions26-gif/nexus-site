Seasonal Surges Overwhelming the Office

One-Time Treatments Never Becoming Recurring Plans

You do a one-time ant treatment for $250. That customer is a perfect candidate for a $75/month quarterly plan. But nobody follows up. They never hear from you again until the ants come back -- and they search Google fresh.

Estimated cost: $1,500 - $2,200/month in missed recurring revenue

After-Hours Emergency Inquiries Going Unanswered

A homeowner finds a wasp nest at 7 PM on Saturday. They message three companies. Two respond Monday morning. One responds in 60 seconds. That one gets the job every time.

Last year you had 180 customers on annual plans. 40 did not renew. Nobody reached out. Those 40 customers are worth $36,000 in annual revenue -- and most would have stayed if someone had simply reminded them.

Estimated cost: $800 - $1,500/month in lapsed renewals
