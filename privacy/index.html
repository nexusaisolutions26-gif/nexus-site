Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-26

Nexus AI Solutions ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit nexusaiconcepts.com or purchase our services.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide: Name, business name, email, phone (optional), and any answers you provide on the AI Readiness Assessment intake form.
  • Payment information: When you purchase a product, payment is processed by our payment processor (Stripe via fastpaydirect.com). We do not store credit card numbers on our systems.
  • Automatically collected: Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, page visits) and analytics events (page views, click attribution via UTM parameters).

How we use information

  • To deliver the products and services you purchase.
  • To send you the assessment intake form, your branded report, and follow-up communication about your account.
  • To send you cold outreach about our services if you appeared on a public business listing as a prospect — you can opt out at any time by replying or emailing us.
  • To improve our website and services based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not share your assessment intake answers with third parties beyond the processors needed to deliver your report (e.g., Anthropic for AI-generated portions where applicable).
  • We do not call you. All communication is asynchronous (email, written messages).

Cookies and tracking

We use minimal analytics. We do not currently run third-party advertising pixels on our site. If we add one in the future, this policy will be updated and a cookie consent banner will be added.

Data retention

We retain customer data for as long as your engagement is active and for a reasonable period afterwards for accounting and audit purposes. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us.

Your rights

  • You can request a copy of all data we hold about you at any time.
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time.
  • You can opt out of cold outreach at any time.
  • You can request a refund per our refund guarantee on the $497 AI Readiness Assessment.

Contact

For any privacy questions, request, or concern, email nexusaisolutions26@gmail.com.
