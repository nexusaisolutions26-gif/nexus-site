How We Fix It for Auto Repair Shops

1

Automated Service Reminder System

Every customer gets reminders at the right interval -- oil changes at 5,000 miles or 6 months, brake inspections annually, tire rotations every 7,500 miles. The system tracks service history and sends text and email reminders before the customer forgets. Shops that automate service reminders see 25-40% higher return visit rates than those relying on customers to remember on their own.

2

Estimate Follow-Up Sequences

When a customer leaves with an open estimate -- a transmission rebuild, an engine repair, a timing belt replacement -- they receive structured follow-up at 48 hours, one week, and two weeks. No pressure, just a reminder that the repair is still needed and booking is easy. Estimate conversion rates climb 20-35% when follow-up is consistent and timely.

3

After-Hours Lead Capture and Response

Breakdowns do not wait for business hours. Your website and messaging channels get an automated response system that acknowledges inquiries within minutes -- day or night. The lead gets confirmation that their request was received, collects vehicle details, and books them into your next available slot. Speed-to-lead wins in auto repair. The first shop to respond gets the job.

4

Review Generation Engine

Two hours after vehicle pickup, customers receive a direct link to leave a Google review. Sent while the relief of a fixed car is still fresh. More reviews means higher local search rankings means more customers choosing you over the dealership service department down the street. The gap closes month after month.

5

Customer Retention and Reactivation

Customers who have not visited in 6, 12, or 18 months receive targeted outreach. Seasonal campaigns for winter prep, summer cooling system checks, and pre-road-trip inspections keep your shop top of mind. Reactivation campaigns typically recover 15-25% of lapsed customers -- turning a quiet database into booked bays.

6

Repair Status Updates

Customers waiting on a repair get automated status updates as work progresses -- vehicle received, diagnosis complete, parts ordered, repair underway, ready for pickup. No more customers wondering what is happening with their car and tying up your service advisors with status inquiries. Fewer inbound questions, higher satisfaction, more five-star reviews.
