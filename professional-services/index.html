Where Professional Services Firms Lose the Most Revenue

Client Inquiries Going Cold

A potential client fills out your contact form at 7 PM. Your office responds the next morning. By then, they have already contacted two other firms and probably hired one. The first firm to respond wins the client 78% of the time.

Estimated cost: $1,500 - $3,500/month in lost clients

Inconsistent Follow-Up After Consultations

A prospect has a great initial consultation but needs time to decide. Your team intends to follow up but gets buried under active casework. Two weeks later, the prospect hired someone who stayed in touch.

Estimated cost: $2,000 - $4,000/month in unconverted consultations

Invoices Aging Past 60 Days

Invoices go out late. Payment reminders are inconsistent. Receivables age past 30, 60, even 90 days. Your firm is floating overhead on uncollected revenue while doing the work that was already completed.

Estimated cost: $800 - $2,000/month in delayed cash flow

Competitors With More Reviews

The firm down the street has 150 Google reviews. You have 22. Their work is not better -- they just ask consistently and make it easy. They rank higher in local search and get more inbound inquiries.

Estimated cost: $1,000 - $2,500/month in lost inbound leads
