What We Build

Seven AI systems.
One operational layer.

We don't sell software. We build custom AI systems connected to your existing tools — and manage them month to month.

01
BI

Business Intelligence Dashboard

Included in Operations+

Your business data currently lives in 3–5 disconnected systems. Every Monday someone spends an hour pulling it together manually. We connect your existing tools — QuickBooks, Jobber, ServiceTitan, HubSpot, or whatever you use — and build one unified live dashboard.

  • Live view: revenue, jobs, leads, invoices, team performance — one screen
  • Automated weekly report delivered to your inbox every Sunday evening
  • Exception alerts: overdue invoices, unanswered leads, missed KPIs flagged automatically
  • Historical comparisons: this week vs. last week, this month vs. last month
  • Accessible on desktop and mobile — check the state of your business in 60 seconds
The result: You know the state of your business in real time, without asking anyone or spending Sunday evening on a spreadsheet.
02
MI

Meeting Intelligence & Action Items

Included in Operations+

Your team meets. Decisions get made. Half of them are forgotten by Thursday. We connect an AI meeting assistant to all your internal meetings — Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, or in-person via phone. Every meeting is automatically transcribed, action items extracted, assigned, and summarized.

  • Full transcript of every meeting — searchable and stored
  • Automatic action item extraction with owner attribution
  • Summary delivered to the owner and relevant team members within minutes
  • Optional: tasks auto-populate into your project management system
  • Works with Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, and phone-based meetings
The result: Nothing falls through the cracks. The same conversation never needs to happen twice.
03
SM

Social Media — Upload & Disappear

Included in all tiers

Your business does great work. Nobody sees it because posting consistently requires time you don't have. We solve this with a dead-simple system: snap a photo of a completed job, send it to a portal with a couple words, and the AI does the rest.

  • 4–5 posts per week across Facebook, Instagram, and Google Business Profile
  • AI-written captions matched to your brand voice and industry
  • Scheduled posting — no manual login, no content calendar to maintain
  • Hashtag optimization and platform-specific formatting handled automatically
  • Monthly content calendar for review if desired
The result: Your social media stays active and professional without anyone managing it.
04
AR

Accounts Receivable Automation

Included in Operations+

Every business has invoices that should have been paid already. Most owners chase them manually, inconsistently, or not at all — because it feels awkward and takes time they don't have. We connect to your invoicing system and build an automated AR follow-up sequence that runs without exception.

  • Automated email + SMS follow-up on all overdue invoices
  • Escalating tone: friendly at day 8, firmer at day 15, final notice at day 22
  • Owner alert when any invoice hits 30 days outstanding
  • Monthly AR summary showing collection rate and outstanding totals
  • Works with QuickBooks, FreshBooks, Jobber, ServiceTitan, and others
The result: You collect more of what you've already earned — without the awkward follow-up calls.
05
LC

Lead & Customer Communication

Included in all tiers

From the moment a lead comes in to the year-two reactivation, every touchpoint is automated and consistent. No lead falls through because someone was busy. No customer goes dark after a job because nobody followed up.

  • Immediate lead response within 2 minutes — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
  • 5-step follow-up sequence for leads that don't book immediately
  • Job confirmation, technician ETA, and completion notifications to customers
  • Automated review requests after every completed job
  • 6-month reactivation sequence for past customers
The result: No lead falls through. Every customer feels taken care of without your team lifting a finger.
06
FT

Field Team Visibility

Included in Operations+

For businesses with field teams, knowing who's where and what got done is a daily challenge. Owners end up making calls, texting techs, or waiting for someone to come back to the office. We automate the visibility layer entirely.

  • Daily end-of-day summary: jobs completed, hours logged, flags raised
  • Customer notification triggers tied to job status (on the way, arrived, completed)
  • Automated job completion follow-up and satisfaction check
  • Weekly team performance summary delivered to managers
  • Exception alerts for missed jobs, late arrivals, or customer complaints
The result: The owner has full visibility without making a single status call.
07
HR

HR & Compliance Administration

Included in Full Business AI

License renewals, certification expirations, onboarding paperwork, employee scheduling — the administrative side of managing people is repetitive, high-stakes, and almost always the thing that falls through the cracks when the owner gets busy.

  • License and certification expiration tracking with 60/30/7-day alerts
  • Insurance renewal reminders well before deadlines
  • Onboarding document routing and completion tracking
  • Scheduling summaries and conflict flagging
  • Annual compliance calendar with automated reminders
The result: Nothing expires without notice. Onboarding runs without the owner tracking it manually.
