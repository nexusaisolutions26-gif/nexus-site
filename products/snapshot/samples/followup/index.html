3. The 4-touch follow-up sequence (templates included)

The sequence is designed to feel like the owner sent each message personally — not a marketing automation. Tone is plain, direct, no "limited time" pressure language.

Touch 1: Day 3 after quote sent

Subject: re: your landscape estimate Hey {{first_name}}, just wanted to follow up on the estimate we sent over Monday for {{project_summary}}. Any questions on the scope, materials, or timeline? Happy to walk through any line item — just hit reply. — {{owner_first_name}} Greenline Landscape

Touch 2: Day 7 after quote sent

Subject: still on for spring? Hey {{first_name}}, our spring book is filling up fast — wanted to make sure you got the estimate and that nothing was unclear. If the timing or budget needs to shift, just let me know what works for you and we'll figure it out. — {{owner_first_name}}

Touch 3: Day 14 after quote sent

Subject: option B? Hey {{first_name}}, I had a chance to think more about your project. If the original scope is more than you want to take on this year, I can put together a phased option (smaller initial scope, larger work next season). Want me to send that over? — {{owner_first_name}}

Touch 4: Day 30 after quote sent (final)

Subject: closing your file Hey {{first_name}}, going to close out your estimate file for now since I haven't heard back. No worries if the timing isn't right — happy to put together a fresh quote whenever you're ready. We're booking into {{next_open_window}} now if you ever want to revisit. — {{owner_first_name}}

4. Install steps for Jobber Connect

  1. Log into Jobber admin at secure.getjobber.com
  2. Settings → Plan → Upgrade to Connect ($179/mo billed annually = $1,548/yr; or $199/mo monthly)
  3. Once upgraded, navigate to Settings → Client Communications → Automated Sequences
  4. Create new sequence: "Quote Follow-up", trigger: "Quote sent, no acceptance after X days"
  5. Add the 4 message templates above as steps 1-4 (Day 3, 7, 14, 30 from quote-sent)
  6. Personalization tokens: use Jobber's built-in {{first_name}}, {{quote_number}}, etc. Custom token {{owner_first_name}} set in Settings → Branding
  7. Test by sending yourself a quote from a test client account. Verify all 4 messages fire on schedule
  8. Turn the sequence ON for all new quotes. Existing quoted-but-not-signed jobs from the last 30 days can be batch-enrolled retroactively (Jobber asks)

6. The 30-day measurement plan

  1. Quote-to-signed conversion rate. Target: 40%+ within 30 days (up from 35%). Tracked natively in Jobber's reports.
  2. Average days from quote-sent to signed. Target: stable or shorter. The sequence shouldn't slow down deals — it should rescue ones that would otherwise vanish.
  3. Reply rate to Touch 1 (day 3). Target: 25%+. If <15%, the message template is too templated-feeling — rewrite in plainer voice.
  4. Unsubscribes / "stop sending" replies. Target: <3% of recipients. If >5%, sequence cadence is too aggressive — extend day-7 to day-10 and day-14 to day-21.
Stop-and-pivot threshold: If after 30 days close-rate hasn't moved at all, the issue isn't follow-up — it's quote pricing or scope. The sequence won't rescue quotes that lost on price. Re-audit your last 20 closed-lost quotes for pricing patterns before assuming the tool isn't working.

Same close-rate impact, more ongoing manual labor. Cost works out lower if quote volume stays under 30/week.
