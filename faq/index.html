About the Service
What exactly does Nexus AI do?
We build custom AI automation systems for businesses with 5-50 employees. Not chatbots. Not another app. We connect to the tools you already use — QuickBooks, Salesforce, HubSpot, whatever — and automate the repetitive administrative work that eats your team's time. Lead response, invoice follow-up, social media, meeting notes, team reporting, compliance tracking. All of it runs automatically without your team managing it.
How is this different from hiring a virtual assistant or office manager?
A virtual assistant works 8 hours a day and handles one thing at a time. Our systems run 24/7, handle everything simultaneously, and never miss a step. A single admin hire costs $35,000-$50,000 per year. Our system costs a fraction of that and does more work. The real difference: an assistant can forget, call in sick, or quit. Automation does not.
Do I need any technical skills to use this?
None. We build everything. We handle the integrations. We manage it monthly. You do not need to learn new software, attend training, or change how your team works. The systems run in the background. You see the results — faster lead response, fewer missed invoices, consistent social media, real-time reporting — without touching the technology.
Will my customers know it is automated?
No. Every message, follow-up, and notification is written in your voice and your brand. Before we build anything, we learn how you talk to customers, what your business values, and what your communication style looks like. The result feels personal because it is based on your actual words and approach. Customers do not know or care that the response came from a system — they care that it came fast and was helpful.
What tools and software do you integrate with?
QuickBooks, FreshBooks, Xero, Salesforce, HubSpot, ServiceTitan, Jobber, Google Business Profile, Facebook, Instagram, Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Gusto, ADP, and hundreds more via direct API connections and Zapier. If your business uses it, we can almost certainly connect to it.
