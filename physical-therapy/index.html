The Four Problems Draining Your PT Practice

Patients Dropping Out Mid-Plan

A patient is prescribed 12 sessions. They attend 4, start feeling better, and vanish. Nobody follows up within the critical 48-hour window. That patient never returns, their outcomes suffer, and your schedule has holes that could have been prevented.

Estimated cost: $1,800 - $3,200/month in incomplete plans of care

No-Shows and Last-Minute Cancellations

A patient cancels at 7 AM for a 9 AM session. Your front desk scrambles to fill the slot manually, but by then the waitlist patients have already made other plans. The session goes empty. Multiply that across your weekly schedule.

Estimated cost: $1,200 - $2,400/month in unfilled sessions

Slow Referral-to-First-Visit Pipeline

A physician sends a referral on Monday. Your office does not reach out until Wednesday. By Friday, the patient has either forgotten, lost motivation, or scheduled with the clinic that contacted them the same day. Every day of delay drops conversion rates significantly.

Estimated cost: $1,000 - $2,000/month in lost new patients

Zero Re-Engagement After Discharge

A patient completes their plan of care and walks out the door. Six months later they tweak the same shoulder again, but they do not think of your clinic because you never followed up. No wellness check-ins, no milestone messages, no seasonal outreach.

Estimated cost: $1,400 - $2,800/month in lost returning patients

How We Fix It for PT Practices

1

Same-Day Referral Response

When a new referral comes in, the patient receives a personalized welcome message within minutes -- not days. The message confirms their referral, explains what to expect at their first visit, and offers easy scheduling options. Practices that respond within the first hour convert referrals at 3x the rate of those that wait 48 hours.

2

Dropout Prevention Sequences

When a patient misses a scheduled session or goes more than 7 days without booking their next visit, they receive automated outreach. The messaging is supportive, not pushy -- acknowledging that life gets busy while reinforcing why completing their plan of care matters. Recovery rate: 30-40% of at-risk patients rebook before fully dropping out.

3

Automated Cancellation Recovery

When a patient cancels, the system immediately reaches out to waitlisted patients to fill the slot. Simultaneously, the cancelling patient gets an easy rebooking option for the same week. No manual scrambling. No lost revenue sitting in empty time blocks.

4

Home Exercise Program Engagement

Between visits, patients receive automated check-ins reminding them to complete their home exercise program. Brief, encouraging messages that keep them engaged with their recovery and connected to your practice between sessions. Compliance rates improve by 25-35% with consistent touchpoints.

5

Post-Discharge Reactivation

After a patient completes their plan of care, they enter an automated long-term nurture sequence. Wellness check-ins at 30, 60, and 90 days. Seasonal injury prevention tips. Anniversary messages. When they need PT again -- or know someone who does -- your practice is the first one they think of.

6

Automated Review Collection

After discharge, patients receive a direct link to leave a Google review while the positive outcome is still top of mind. More five-star reviews means higher local search visibility, which means more referrals finding your practice first. The compound effect grows every month.
