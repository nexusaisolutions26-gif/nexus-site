The Revenue Killers in Your Service Business

  • After-hours leads vanish overnight. HVAC and plumbing emergencies do not wait for business hours. When a homeowner reaches out at night or on a weekend, they expect a fast response. If your office is closed and there is no system in place, that lead contacts the next company on the list. The job is gone before Monday.
  • Your office staff cannot keep up during peak season. When summer heat or winter cold hits, your phones ring nonstop. Your dispatcher is juggling scheduling, customer questions, and follow-ups all at once. Leads slip through. Existing customers wait on hold. Service quality drops when your team is overwhelmed.
  • No-shows and last-minute cancellations wreck your schedule. A technician drives to a job site and nobody is home. Or the customer cancels thirty minutes before the appointment. That is a truck roll wasted, a time slot lost, and revenue that cannot be recovered. Without reminders and confirmations, this happens far too often.
  • Seasonal revenue swings leave you scrambling. You are slammed in the summer and winter, then revenue drops in spring and fall. Without proactive outreach -- maintenance reminders, seasonal tune-up campaigns, service agreements -- you are entirely dependent on inbound demand and weather patterns.
  • You do great work but have too few reviews. Your technicians deliver excellent service. Customers are happy. But almost none of them leave a review unless prompted. Meanwhile, a competitor with more reviews and higher visibility on Google is getting the leads that should be yours.

What Nexus AI Builds for HVAC and Plumbing Companies

24/7 Lead Response

Every lead -- web form, chat, text message, social media inquiry -- receives an immediate, professional response regardless of time or day. The system captures the service need, provides relevant information, and moves the customer toward booking. No lead waits until morning.

Automated Scheduling and Dispatch Support

Customers are guided to available appointment slots without tying up your dispatcher. Confirmations go out immediately. Reminders are sent at 24 hours and 2 hours before the appointment. Cancellations trigger automatic rebooking outreach to fill the gap.

Seasonal Campaign Automation

Before summer and winter peaks, your existing customer base receives targeted outreach for tune-ups, maintenance agreements, and seasonal inspections. These campaigns run automatically, filling your schedule during shoulder seasons and locking in work before the rush.

Service Agreement and Maintenance Reminders

Customers with maintenance agreements receive automated reminders when their service is due. Customers without agreements receive periodic offers to sign up. This builds recurring revenue and keeps your trucks moving year-round instead of only during peak demand.

Post-Job Review Requests

After every completed service call, the customer receives a timely request to share their experience. Positive responses are directed to Google and other review platforms. Negative feedback is captured privately so you can resolve it before it becomes a public complaint.

Customer Reactivation Campaigns

Customers who have not used your services in over a year receive targeted reactivation messages. The system identifies dormant customers and re-engages them with relevant offers, bringing back revenue that would otherwise be permanently lost to a competitor.
