What Is Costing Your Firm Cases Right Now

  • Slow lead response is killing your conversion rate. Legal consumers contact multiple firms at once. Research consistently shows that the first firm to respond captures the majority of new clients. If your intake team takes hours to follow up, you are handing retained clients to your competitors on a silver platter.
  • Your intake process leaks qualified leads. A potential client reaches out. Your receptionist takes a message. The message sits on someone's desk. A day goes by. The follow-up call happens too late -- or not at all. There is no system ensuring every inquiry is captured, qualified, and moved through a consistent intake pipeline.
  • Consultation no-shows waste attorney time. You block an hour for a consultation. The prospect does not show. No reminder was sent. No confirmation was requested. Your attorney sat idle during time that could have been billed or used for another meeting. Across a month, the lost hours add up fast.
  • Clients feel left in the dark on their cases. Once a client retains you, weeks can go by without communication. They start calling and emailing for updates, pulling your staff away from substantive work. Or worse, they leave a negative review because they felt ignored -- even though their case was progressing normally.
  • Your online reputation does not reflect your results. You have won significant cases and built a strong track record. But your Google listing has a handful of reviews, most of them old. Prospective clients searching for an attorney see competitors with more reviews and more recent feedback, and they choose accordingly.

What Nexus AI Builds for Law Firms

Instant Lead Engagement

Every inquiry -- web form, chat message, social media DM -- receives an immediate, professional response. The system captures case details, qualifies the lead based on your practice areas, and moves them toward a consultation. Response time drops from hours to seconds.

Structured Intake Pipeline

New leads enter a defined pipeline with stages, automatic follow-ups, and clear accountability. No more sticky notes or lost messages. Every potential client is tracked from first contact through retained, with automated nudges at each stage to keep them moving forward.

Consultation Confirmations and Reminders

When a consultation is scheduled, the prospect receives an immediate confirmation followed by reminders at strategic intervals. If they need to reschedule, the system handles it without involving your staff. No-shows drop significantly when every appointment is confirmed in advance.

Automated Case Status Updates

Clients receive regular, automated updates on their case progress at intervals you define. When a case moves to a new stage, the client is notified. This reduces inbound status calls, improves client satisfaction, and protects your reputation from complaints about poor communication.

Review Generation System

After a case concludes successfully, the client receives a simple, well-timed request to share their experience. Positive feedback is directed to Google and other review platforms. Concerns are captured privately so you can address them directly. Your online reputation grows consistently.

Referral and Re-engagement Campaigns

Past clients receive periodic touchpoints that keep your firm top of mind. When they or someone they know needs legal help again, your firm is the first they think of. Referral-based leads close at higher rates and cost nothing to acquire.
