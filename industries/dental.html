The Problems Every Dental Practice Knows Too Well

  • New patient inquiries sit unanswered. Someone fills out your website form at 8 PM or calls during lunch. By the time your front desk follows up the next morning, they have already booked with another practice. Speed-to-lead in dental is measured in minutes, not days.
  • No-shows destroy your production numbers. A patient books a crown prep worth several hundred dollars. No confirmation goes out. They forget. That chair sits empty for ninety minutes while your hygienist and assistant wait. Multiply that by four or five no-shows per week and the lost production is staggering.
  • Recall patients disappear. Patients who need their six-month cleaning simply stop coming. Your recall list grows, but nobody has time to work it. Every lapsed recall patient represents years of lost hygiene revenue and potential restorative work that never gets diagnosed.
  • Online reviews trickle in slowly. Your happiest patients walk out the door and never think to leave a review. Meanwhile, the one patient who had a bad day posts a one-star review that sits at the top of your Google listing. Your reputation does not reflect the quality of care you deliver.
  • The front desk is buried. Your receptionist answers calls, checks patients in, verifies insurance, processes payments, and somehow is also supposed to follow up on treatment plans, work the recall list, and respond to online inquiries. Something always falls through the cracks.

What Nexus AI Builds for Dental Practices

Instant Lead Response

When a prospective patient submits a form or sends a message, they receive an immediate, personalized response. The system captures their information, answers common questions about your practice, and moves them toward booking -- all within seconds, day or night.

Smart Appointment Confirmations

Every scheduled patient receives automated confirmations and reminders via text and email at the right intervals. Patients can confirm with a single tap. If they need to reschedule, the system handles it and opens that slot for someone on your waitlist.

Automated Recall and Reactivation

Patients due for hygiene appointments receive a sequence of friendly, well-timed messages that bring them back. Patients who have not visited in over a year get a separate reactivation campaign. Your recall list works itself instead of gathering dust.

Review Generation on Autopilot

After every appointment, patients receive a simple prompt asking about their experience. Happy patients are guided directly to your Google or Facebook review page. Negative feedback is captured privately so you can resolve it before it goes public.

Treatment Follow-Up Sequences

When a patient is presented with a treatment plan but does not schedule, the system follows up automatically. It addresses common concerns, reinforces the value of the recommended care, and makes it easy for the patient to book when they are ready.

Centralized Communication Hub

All patient messages -- text, email, web chat, social media -- flow into one dashboard. Your team sees every conversation in one place. No more toggling between platforms, no more messages slipping through the cracks.
