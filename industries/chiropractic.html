The Growth Killers in Your Chiropractic Practice

  • New patient inquiries go unanswered for hours. When someone is in pain and looking for relief, they are not waiting around. If your front desk is busy with in-office patients and cannot respond quickly, that prospective patient moves on to the next practice in the search results.
  • Patients drop off their care plan and never come back. A patient starts a treatment plan, feels some relief after a few visits, and stops showing up. Without consistent follow-up and re-engagement, those patients disappear -- along with the revenue from the remaining visits on their plan.
  • No-shows leave gaps in your schedule and money on the table. A patient forgets their appointment or decides not to come without canceling. That is a time slot that could have been filled by someone else. Without automated reminders and confirmations, no-shows eat into your daily revenue.
  • Your front desk is stretched too thin. Answering inquiries, checking patients in, verifying information, handling follow-up messages -- your front desk team is doing the work of three people. When they are overwhelmed, new patient outreach suffers and existing patient communication slows down.
  • You rely on word of mouth but have few online reviews. Your patients get great results. They tell friends and family. But they rarely take the time to leave a Google review. Without a system that consistently asks for reviews after positive visits, your online presence does not reflect the quality of care you deliver.

What Nexus AI Builds for Chiropractic Practices

Instant New Patient Response

Every inquiry -- web form, text, chat, social media message -- receives an immediate, professional response at any hour. The system captures the patient's concern, provides relevant information about your practice, and guides them toward scheduling their first visit.

Automated Appointment Reminders

Patients receive confirmation messages when they schedule and reminders before their appointment. Missed appointments trigger automatic re-engagement to get the patient back on the books. Your schedule stays full and no-shows drop significantly.

Care Plan Follow-Up Sequences

When a patient misses a visit or falls off their care plan, automated follow-up messages re-engage them with personalized outreach. The system keeps patients on track with their treatment, improving outcomes and protecting the revenue tied to their plan.

Post-Visit Review Generation

After visits, patients receive a timely request to share their experience. Positive responses are directed to Google and review platforms. Negative feedback is captured privately so you can resolve concerns before they become public complaints.

Patient Reactivation Campaigns

Patients who have not visited in months receive targeted outreach encouraging them to return. The system identifies inactive patients and re-engages them with relevant messaging, recovering revenue that would otherwise be permanently lost.

Referral and Wellness Campaigns

Automated campaigns educate your patient base on wellness topics and encourage referrals. These sequences run in the background, keeping your practice top of mind and generating new patient leads from your existing satisfied patients.
