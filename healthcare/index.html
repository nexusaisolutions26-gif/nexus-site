The Four Problems Draining Your Practice

No-Shows That Never Get Rebooked

A patient misses their Tuesday appointment. Nobody follows up the same day. By Wednesday, the patient has forgotten. That slot is gone and so is the revenue. Across a month, this adds up fast.

Estimated cost: $3,000 - $8,000/month

New Patient Inquiries Going Cold

A potential patient fills out a form on your website at 7 PM. Your front desk responds the next morning. They already booked with the practice that responded within 5 minutes.

Estimated cost: $1,500 - $3,500/month

Front Desk Drowning in Admin

Your front desk staff spends 60% of their time on intake forms, insurance calls, appointment reminders, and rescheduling. That is time not spent greeting patients, answering questions, or managing the flow of the office.

Estimated cost: $1,200 - $2,500/month in overhead

Patients Falling Off the Recall List

Patients who need to come back for follow-ups, cleanings, or ongoing treatment simply disappear. Without systematic outreach, 30-40% of patients due for recall never rebook.

Estimated cost: $2,000 - $4,000/month in lost recurring revenue

How We Fix It for Healthcare Practices

1

Instant Patient Inquiry Response

Every new patient inquiry gets a personalized acknowledgment within 60 seconds -- day, night, or weekend. The message confirms what they asked for, tells them what happens next, and gives them confidence they chose the right practice. Your front desk follows up with a warm lead instead of a cold one.

2

Three-Touch Appointment Reminders

72-hour email confirmation. 24-hour text reminder with one-tap confirm or reschedule. 2-hour final reminder for same-day appointments. This sequence reduces no-shows by 30-50% compared to a single reminder. 98% of texts are opened within 3 minutes.

3

Automated No-Show Recovery

When a patient misses an appointment, they receive an automated rebooking message within 30 minutes. No judgment, no pressure -- just an easy way to reschedule. Recovery rate: 40-60% of no-shows rebook. That is thousands in monthly revenue that would have been zero.

4

Pre-Visit Intake Automation

New patients receive intake forms, insurance verification requests, and welcome information before they walk in. Check-in time drops from 15 minutes to under 3. Your front desk is freed up to focus on the patients who are actually in the office.

5

Recall and Reactivation System

Patients due for follow-ups, cleanings, or ongoing treatment get automated outreach at exactly the right time. No more pulling recall lists manually. Practices that automate recall see 25-35% higher patient retention rates.

6

Automated Review Collection

Two hours after checkout, patients receive a direct link to leave a Google review. Sent while the experience is fresh. More reviews means higher local search rankings means more new patient inquiries. The cycle compounds every month.
