Estimated cost: $2,000 - $3,000/month in lost future deals

Manual Transaction Coordination Eating Hours

Your agents spend 10-15 hours per week chasing signatures, confirming deadlines, updating clients on milestones, and managing inspection and appraisal schedules. That is time not spent generating new business or serving clients.

How We Fix It for Real Estate

1

Instant Lead Response

Every new lead from Zillow, Realtor.com, your website, or social media gets a personalized response within 60 seconds -- day, night, or weekend. The message acknowledges their specific inquiry, answers their immediate question, and books a session directly on your calendar. Your agents follow up with a warm conversation instead of a cold introduction.

2

Long-Term Nurture Sequences

Leads that are 3, 6, or 12 months out from buying or selling enter automated drip sequences with market updates, neighborhood insights, and timely check-ins. When they are ready to move, your name is the one they remember. Nurture sequences convert 15-25% of long-term leads that would otherwise be lost entirely.

3

Transaction Coordination Automation

From accepted offer to closing day, every milestone triggers automated client updates, deadline reminders, and document requests. Inspection scheduled -- client notified. Appraisal complete -- next steps sent. Your agents save 10-15 hours per week per active transaction while clients feel more informed than ever.

4

Post-Close Follow-Up System

After closing, clients receive a thank-you message, a 7-day check-in, a 30-day settling-in message, a 90-day review request, and annual home anniversary outreach. This keeps you top of mind and turns every closed deal into a referral engine that compounds year over year.

5

Review and Referral Generation

At the right moment after closing, clients receive a direct link to leave a Google review plus a simple referral prompt. Sent when satisfaction is highest. More 5-star reviews means higher visibility in local search, and direct referral asks convert at 3-5x the rate of hoping clients remember on their own.

6

Market Update Campaigns

Automated monthly or biweekly market reports sent to your entire database -- segmented by area, price range, and buyer/seller intent. Positions you as the local expert, keeps your name in front of past clients and prospects, and generates inbound inquiries from people who are ready to move now.
