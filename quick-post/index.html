Posting cadence — don't blast all in one day:
  • Day 1 (Tue): LinkedIn long post
  • Day 2 (Wed): Facebook
  • Day 3 (Thu): X — pick single tweet OR 4-tweet thread, not both
  • Day 4 (Fri): Hacker News submission (Tue or Thu morning is better if you can wait)
  • Day 5+: Reply to any comments. That's where the conversions happen, not the post.

Why this works at all: the cornerstone post is the bait, not the products. People click for the story, land on the blog post, see the products link in the body, and a small % convert. Spamming the products link directly without the story = ignored.
