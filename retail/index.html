A customer adds items to their cart and leaves. Nobody follows up. No reminder email. No incentive to return. That sale is gone permanently, and it happens dozens of times per week across most retail businesses.

Zero Post-Purchase Follow-Up

A customer buys once and never hears from you again. No thank-you message, no product recommendations, no check-in. They forget you exist by the time they need something again and buy from whoever shows up first.

How We Fix It for Retail Businesses

1

Abandoned Cart Recovery Sequences

When a customer abandons their cart, they receive a personalized reminder within one hour. A second message follows at 24 hours with a subtle urgency cue. A third at 72 hours can include a time-limited incentive. Recovery rate: 10-15% of abandoned carts convert. On a store doing $20K/month, that is $1,500-$2,000 in recovered revenue every single month.

2

Post-Purchase Follow-Up Sequences

Every customer receives a thank-you message after purchase, a delivery or pickup confirmation, a product care or usage tip at day 3, and a review request at day 7. This keeps your brand top of mind and turns one-time buyers into repeat customers. Stores with post-purchase automation see 25-40% higher repeat purchase rates.

3

Loyalty Program Automation

Automated point tracking, milestone rewards, and VIP tier notifications. Customers get notified when they are close to a reward, when they level up, and when exclusive offers are available. No manual tracking. No forgotten rewards. Loyalty programs with automation see 2-3x higher engagement than manual programs.

4

Seasonal Campaign Automation

Pre-built campaign sequences for every major retail season -- automatically deployed at the right time with the right messaging. Early access for VIPs, countdown sequences for the general list, and last-chance reminders. No more scrambling the week before a holiday. Your campaigns run themselves.

5

Automated Review Generation

Seven days after purchase, customers receive a direct link to leave a Google review. Timed perfectly for when they have used the product and formed an opinion. More reviews means higher local search visibility and more trust with new shoppers. The cycle compounds monthly.

6

Inventory and Restock Alerts

When popular items are back in stock or new arrivals match a customer's purchase history, they get an automated notification. Customers who opted into restock alerts convert at 3-5x higher rates than cold traffic. This turns your inventory updates into a revenue channel.
