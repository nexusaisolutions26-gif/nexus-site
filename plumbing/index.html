The Four Problems Draining Your Plumbing Business

After-Hours Emergency Leads Going Unanswered

A homeowner has a burst pipe at 10 PM. They submit a form on your site. Your office responds at 8 AM. By then, they already booked with the first company that replied -- 11 hours ago.

Estimated cost: $1,800 - $3,200/month

Estimates That Never Get Followed Up

Your tech writes up a $4,500 repipe estimate. It goes to the customer. No one follows up at day 3, day 7, or day 14. The customer forgets, gets another quote, or just does nothing. That revenue vanishes.

Estimated cost: $1,500 - $2,800/month

Scheduling Gaps and Dispatch Inefficiency

Your plumber finishes a water heater install at 10:30. Next job is at 1 PM. That is 2.5 hours of dead time you are paying for. Across a crew of 6, the idle hours add up to thousands in wasted labor every month.

Estimated cost: $1,200 - $2,000/month

Zero Review Generation Strategy

Plumbers live and die by Google reviews. Your team finishes 80+ jobs a month but maybe 2 customers leave a review unprompted. Meanwhile, your competitor with half your skills has twice your rating because they ask every time.

Estimated cost: $1,000 - $1,800/month in lost new business
